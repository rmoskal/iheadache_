Tracking Custom Symptoms

We ask the specific questions about your headaches because they are used to help determine the type of headache (migraine, tension, etc.) you are having.  Users have requested that we add “custom symptom tracking” so we have done this!  You can add an unlimited number of “Custom Symptoms” to track. 

Keep in mind you will still have to answer our typical symptoms questions in order for iHeadache to determine the headache type.

Adding, Inactivating and Deleteing Custom Symptoms

Many users desire to track symptoms that are not included on the standard symptom list. 

  • Add a custom symptom - click the “add” button and enter the symptom. 
  • If you want to stop tracking a custom symptom but may want to follow it later, uncheck the include box. 
  • This is not recommended but if you do not wish to ever use a custom symptom again then you may delete the symptom by selecting the entire line and clicking the delete button.  Beware: deleting this custom symptom may make the reports inaccurate from the time period that you used to track this custom symptom.  Is really best to just choose to “not include” in your profile by “unchecking” the box.
