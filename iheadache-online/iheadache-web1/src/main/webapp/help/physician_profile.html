Allowing Your Physican(s) to View Your Diary

(My Profile Tab >Physicians)

We have had many of our the physician's who reccomed iHeadache to their patients ask about having access to the electronic data from iHeadache.  This way they can cut/paste data or import your entire report to their Electronic Medical Records (EMR).

Adding your Physician/Provider  (is this how it is supposed to work?)

To allow a physician or provider to look at your headache data then you can add your name and data to be included in their patient list. 

  • Click the “add” button and a list of physician/providers will open.  Double click on the name you want to add.  You can also search for a provider by entering data in the “search box”.
  • You can add multiple providers.  For example if you see a physician and their nurse provider then you can add them both. 
  • The providers will get an email that you requested access and then can “confirm” or “decline” you as their patient.

If your provider is not on our list

If your physician is not on this list, then he has not registered with iHeadache.  Please click here and fill out this online form and we can contact them about becoming registered. Or you can download a brochure to take to him.    (need link to both)
